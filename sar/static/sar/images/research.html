Dikika Research Project

Fossil field research in Ethiopia

In the remote Afar triangle of northeastern Ethiopia we have discovered spectacular fossil remains of a young Australopithecus afarensis. My work examines the micromammalian fauna associated with this fossil and the paleoecological context of our early adaptation and evolution. Our study area, called Dikika, is remarkably fossiliferous and every season yields exciting fossil discoveries.
